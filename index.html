Forskaren - Urban Deli

Bespoke Furniture & Interior Design

Forskaren is a cutting-edge life science center in Hagastaden, Stockholm, developed by Vectura Fastigheter and designed by 3XN. The ground floor features two public restaurants that also serve as lunch spots for building occupants. Urban Deli who will operate the two restaurants hired my team at This Way Up as consultants to take part in developing the interior concept and material selection. A key element is the main staircase, centrally located in one of the restaurants, serving multiple functions. We chose to highlight it with a bold, distinctive material choice.
