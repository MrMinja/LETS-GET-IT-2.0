Forskaren - Urban Deli


Bespoke Furniture & Interior Design


Interior Design (2024) Forskaren is a cutting-edge life science center in Hagastaden, Stockholm, developed by Vectura Fastigheter and designed by 3XN. The ground floor features two public restaurants that also serve as lunch spots for building occupants. Urban Deli who will operate the two restaurants hired my team at This Way Up as consultants to take part in developing the interior concept and material selection. A key element is the main staircase, centrally located in one of the restaurants, serving multiple functions. We chose to highlight it with a bold, distinctive material choice.

The staircase features custom-made terrazzo, covering the sides, stairs, and pathways between seating areas. Accented by brown leather and brushed steel, the aesthetic is cool, bold, and sophisticated, reflecting the building's exclusive material palette. To meet high accessibility a, careful consideration was given to the size of the terrazzo chips. Larger blue chips (3-5 cm) for the sides and smaller ones (1 cm) for the walking paths. This deliberate adjustment maximizes contrast and accessibility. My role included, among various things, establishing contact with the terrazzo manufacturers, research, 3D visualization, and concept development.
