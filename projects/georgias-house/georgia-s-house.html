Spacial Design


This project evolved from a preparatory art school assignment to design a space dedicated to someone admired. I chose to create a meditation house for the modernist painter Georgia O'Keeffe (1887-1986). To capture O'Keeffe's artistic spirit and her connection with nature and simplicity, I incorporated the following key elements into the design

I designed the house as an outbuilding on Georgia O'Keeffe's New Mexico ranch, offering a serene space for inspiration and contemplation. The house is modest, lacking plumbing and traditional dwelling functions, and rests on pillars to minimize its impact on the landscape. To counter the intense sun and dry air, large sliding doors and windows connect the indoor and outdoor spaces, providing panoramic views of the surrounding and a seamless connection between the indoor and outdoor spaces.
