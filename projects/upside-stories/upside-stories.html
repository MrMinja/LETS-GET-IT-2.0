Upside Stories


Concept & Interior Design


The concept focused on using existing materials to create an inviting showroom that captures the interest of passersby. Inspired by the site's industrial history, the design incorporated vibrant accent colors and rough materials.

In addition to contributing to concept development, I produced technical drawings for the carpenters, created visualization materials and descriptions for the client, and sourced secondhand furniture and fixtures. Given the water damage on the building's exterior, we proposed renewing it with galvanized tin sheets mounted over the existing facade. This solution is cost-effective, time-efficient, and allows for easy disassembly and reuse.
